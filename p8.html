Model 2: Witch's Brew


The original teapot was the easiest to make, as there was a tutorial to follow. The only hardship I had creating this teapot was using the appearance to make it original. I decided on making a teapot inspired by the genie lamp from Aladdin using a tarnished brass material. The second model was much harder. I realized that parts of my sketch would not be able to translate directly into Fusion 360. Since the revolve function was used to create both the body and the lid of the teapot, they had to be symmetrical. My design was not. I had decided to sketch out a teapot inspired by a cauldron, with a witch hat as the lid. The witch hat was not symmetrical, which proved to be troublesome in the final model, leading to modifications. After resolving this complication, the rest of the modeling went smoothly.
